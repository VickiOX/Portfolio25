Danske Spil

Client project - Digital Concept developer

Purpose

Danske Spil’s purpose is not only to offer gambling services, but to do so in a way that prioritizes player well-being and the greater good of society. Responsibility is a core value embedded in the company’s foundation, particularly reflected in its dedicated efforts to prevent gambling addiction and protect vulnerable players.


Problem Statement

NoA Ignite has formulated the following problem statement for Danske Spil: "How can responsibility be expressed by Danske Spil?" Based on this problem statement, we have developed the following research questions that we aim to explore:

  • What responsible initiatives does Danske Spil have?
  • What responsible initiatives can be found in similar companies abroad?
  • Who becomes addicted to gambling?
  • Why do they start, and why do they become addicted?
  • Does the target group perceive Danske Spil’s responsible initiatives?


Development Process

The development process followed the Double Diamond model, which structures the design work into four interconnected phases.

  • In the Discover phase, an open and exploratory approach was used to gain insight into the users’ needs and the broader problem. This included desk research, interviews, and a survey.

  • In the Define phase, the collected insights were analyzed and narrowed down to clearly identify the core challenge.

  • During the Develop phase, ideas and potential solutions were generated and tested. The first prototype was created using Figma and tested with the target group.

  • Finally, in the Deliver phase, the best solution was refined and further developed into a second prototype, also created in Figma.


The Concept

Vi griber dig, før ludomanien gør (We catch you before gambling addiction does) is a preventive concept targeting young men aged 18–24 who show early signs of problematic gambling. The initiative integrates responsible gaming tools directly into Danske Spil’s Oddset app—the most used platform by the target group.

At registration, users aged 18–24 are asked to provide a trusted contact. If they exceed their personalized Max Spending limit, both the user and the contact are notified. The contact must confirm their role via a two-step verification.

Users set a Max Spending limit based on suggested income levels. This limit is visualized with a piggy bank graphic that changes color depending on usage. Exceeding the limit triggers a warning with a video featuring a relatable young figure sharing their gambling struggles, plus a checklist of addiction signs and a direct link to get help.

To encourage healthy habits, a points system rewards users who stay within their Max Spending. Rewards include discounts and experiences through Danske Spil’s partners like DBU and Carlsberg. These measures aim to support responsible gambling behavior and are deactivated when the user turns 25.
