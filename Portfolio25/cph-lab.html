CPH-LAB

Exam Project - Multimedia Designer (UX/UI Designer)

Purpose

The purpose of the CPH-LAB website redesign is to strengthen the company’s identity and support its business by creating a more user-friendly, accessible, and reliable platform. Based on thorough research, the new design aims to reflect CPH-LAB’s passion for analog photography as a craft worth preserving, while improving communication with both experienced users and newcomers. The solution enhances the order process, integrates clear and engaging design choices, and highlights values like sustainability and accessibility to deliver a consistent and authentic user experience.


Problem Statement

How can we optimize CPH-LAB’s development purchase process and information flow to ensure user-friendliness and a streamlined design?


Development Process

The development process followed the Design Thinking process, which structures the design work into six interconnected phases.

  • In the Empathize phase, a user-focused approach was applied to understand the target group's experiences, challenges, and needs. This involved observations, interviews, and other forms of user research.

  • In the Define phase, the gathered insights were analyzed to clearly frame the core problem, ensuring the team addressed the real user needs.

  • In the Ideate phase, a wide range of creative ideas were developed to solve the defined problem. Brainstorming techniques were used to explore different directions.

  • In the Prototype phase, selected ideas were translated into low-fidelity prototypes, allowing for early testing and feedback.

  • In the Test phase,the prototypes were evaluated with users, and their feedback was used to refine and improve the solution.

  • Finally, in the Implement phase, the final solution was developed and prepared for real-world use. This included final refinements and integration into the intended platform or service.


The Solution

The solution is a redesigned and optimized website for CPH-LAB, aimed at improving usability and creating a streamlined, intuitive design. The new platform makes analog photography more accessible to both experienced users and newcomers by simplifying the development process and presenting information in a clear and engaging way example by a scrolly telling in the OM (about) page.

It addresses the issue of the site not effectively communicating with users unfamiliar with analog photography, and introduces a more transparent and user-friendly ordering process. The redesign strengthens CPH-LAB’s brand and identity by visually and functionally reflecting the company's values and passion for analog photography as an authentic craft worth preserving.

The solution is developed in line with design principles and industry standards, with a strong focus on sustainability and accessibility. Ultimately, the website becomes a reliable and inviting digital touchpoint that enhances the customer journey and supports business growth.
